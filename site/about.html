Flu View Help

About

I got the idea from FLUTRACK. Using twitter to track potiential flu outbreaks by seeing where people were tweeting about their flu symptoms. I wanted to expand on this idea and plot a heatmap showing potential outbreaks based on the data returned.

Originally, I wanted to expand this even further and search twitter directly for various topics and plot the heatmap onto Google maps. For example, if you searched for "Trump" or "Immigration" what would the heatmap look like across the US or the world? Would you see more activity inside the US for those topics or outside?

Unfortunately the majority of tweets don't appear to have latitude and longitude associated with them. I believe I could still get reasonable results by using the location data stored at the user level, but this would have required more time than I currenly have for this project.
